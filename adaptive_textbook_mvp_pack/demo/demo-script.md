# Three-minute demo

1. Open the triangle-area chapter.
2. Select the formula block.
3. Ask: “Why do we divide by two?”
4. Show the answer and its block references.
5. Choose the wrong quiz option: “Any side connected to the top vertex.”
6. Show the detected misconception.
7. Wait for the personalized note and practice question to appear.
8. Toggle Original / My Textbook.
9. Export Markdown.
10. Close with: “We are not putting a chatbot beside a textbook. We are making the textbook evolve with the student.”
